## About

Dong Lao is an Assistant Professor in the Division of Computer Science and Engineering within the **LSU** College of Engineering. Prior to LSU, he was a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at the **UCLA** Vision Lab under Prof. Stefano Soatto. He holds a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences from **KAUST** under Prof. Ganesh Sundaramoorthi and a B.S. in Mathematics from **SJTU**.

His research focuses on computer vision models with minimal human supervision, robustness of AI, computational imaging, and optimization. His work appears in CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICML, and JMLR.
