About

Dong Lao is an Assistant Professor in the Division of Computer Science and Engineering within the LSU College of Engineering. Prior to LSU, he was a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at UCLA Vision Lab. He holds a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences from KAUST and a B.S. in Mathematics from SJTU.

His research focuses on computer vision models with minimal human supervision, robustness of AI, computational imaging, and optimization. His work appears in CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICML, and JMLR.
